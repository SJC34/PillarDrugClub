Loading the script 'https://js.stripe.com/basil/stripe.js' violates 
Content Security Policy directive: "script-src 'self' 'unsafe-inline' 'unsafe-eval'"


Your Content Security Policy is blocking Stripe from loading. This means payment processing is broken for crawlers and potentially some users. This is a production issue, not just a crawl issue.
Fix: Add https://js.stripe.com to the script-src directive in your CSP header. Current CSP:

script-src 'self' 'unsafe-inline' 'unsafe-eval'


Needs to be:

script-src 'self' 'unsafe-inline' 'unsafe-eval' https://js.stripe.com https://*.stripe.com


Also add to frame-src (Stripe uses iframes):

frame-src 'self' https://js.squareup.com https://*.stripe.com

# Pillar Drug Club — LegitScript Compliance Overview

## 1. Business Model

Pillar Drug Club (PDC) operates as a **membership-based pharmacy purchasing service**. For $99 per year, members gain access to wholesale pricing on FDA-approved generic medications. PDC does not dispense medications, hold pharmacy licenses, or maintain pharmaceutical inventory.

### What PDC Does
- Provides a digital platform for members to browse FDA-approved generic medications at wholesale prices.
- Facilitates prescription requests by generating PDF documents that are faxed to prescribing physicians for authorization.
- Transmits verified prescriptions to the licensed fulfillment pharmacy for dispensing and shipping.
- Offers cost comparison tools so members can see potential savings versus typical retail pharmacy pricing.

### What PDC Does Not Do
- PDC does not dispense, package, compound, or ship medications.
- PDC does not prescribe medications or provide medical advice.
- PDC does not sell or offer controlled substances (Schedules I–V).
- PDC does not import medications from outside the United States.
- PDC does not sell compounded or non-FDA-approved medications.
- PDC does not make unverified health claims or guarantee specific outcomes.

## 2. Fulfillment Partner

All medication dispensing and shipping is handled by **HealthWarehouse**, a fully licensed mail-order pharmacy.

| Credential | Details |
|---|---|
| VIPPS Accreditation | Verified Internet Pharmacy Practice Sites (NABP) |
| LegitScript Certification | Independently certified by LegitScript |
| DEA License | Licensed for controlled substance handling (PDC does not offer controlled substances) |
| State Pharmacy Licenses | Licensed in all 50 US states |
| Physical Location | Florence, Kentucky, USA |

PDC integrates with HealthWarehouse via their API to transmit orders and track shipment status. All dispensing decisions, pharmacist reviews, and drug utilization reviews (DUR) are performed by HealthWarehouse's licensed pharmacists.

---

## 3. Prescription Requirements

PDC enforces the following prescription safeguards:

1. **No prescriptions are generated by PDC.** All prescriptions must originate from a licensed prescriber (MD, DO, NP, PA) with an active NPI.
2. **Prescription Request Workflow:** Members submit a prescription request through the platform specifying their medication, prescribing doctor's name/phone/fax, and any special instructions. PDC generates a PDF and faxes it to the prescriber's office for authorization.
3. **No dispensing without verification.** HealthWarehouse independently verifies every prescription before dispensing.
4. **Prescription transfer support.** Members may transfer existing prescriptions from other pharmacies. Transfers follow standard pharmacy transfer procedures.
5. **Refill management.** Refill requests are submitted through the platform and routed to the fulfillment pharmacy for processing within the bounds of the original prescription authorization.

## 4. Medication Catalog

- **Generic medications only.** All medications in the PDC catalog are FDA-approved generic drugs.
- **No controlled substances.** PDC explicitly excludes Schedule I–V controlled substances from its catalog.
- **No compounded medications.** All medications are commercially manufactured by FDA-registered manufacturers.
- **No imported or gray-market medications.** All medications are sourced from licensed US wholesalers through HealthWarehouse's supply chain.
- **Pricing transparency.** Wholesale prices are displayed alongside typical retail prices. All savings claims are qualified with "up to" language and cite verifiable sources (e.g., AAM, GoodRx published pricing).

- Savings claims use "up to" qualifiers (e.g., "Save Up to 89%") with source attribution.
- Comparison pricing references published data from the Association for Accessible Medicines (AAM) and publicly available GoodRx pricing.

### Claims NOT Made
- No claim of being a pharmacy, drugstore, or dispensary.
- No claim of having licensed pharmacists on staff (pharmacists are employed by HealthWarehouse).
- No guarantee of specific health outcomes.

## 6. Privacy & Data Handling (HIPAA Alignment)

### Technical Safeguards
- **Encryption in transit:** All data transmitted over HTTPS/TLS.
- **Password security:** Passwords are hashed with bcrypt (10 rounds). Password policy enforces minimum complexity requirements.
- **Session management:** Sessions use secure, httpOnly cookies with configurable expiration.
- **Account lockout:** Accounts are temporarily locked after repeated failed login attempts (HIPAA Security Rule alignment).
- **Audit logging:** All authentication events, PHI access, and administrative actions are logged with timestamp, user ID, IP address, and action type.

### Data Minimization
- Only data necessary for prescription fulfillment is collected (name, DOB, address, medication, prescriber info, allergies).
- Payment processing is handled by Stripe — PDC does not store credit card numbers.
- Drug allergy data is stored to support drug interaction and adverse reaction screening (via OpenFDA integration).

### Policies Published on the Website
- **Privacy Policy** (`/privacy-policy`) — Describes data collection, use, sharing, and retention practices.
- **Terms of Service** (`/terms-of-service`) — Defines the membership agreement, service limitations, and dispute resolution.
- **Refund Policy** (`/refund-policy`) — 30-day money-back guarantee on the membership fee; medication refunds follow HealthWarehouse's policy.

## 9. End-to-End Patient Workflow

The following describes the complete user journey from registration through order receipt.

### Step 1: Registration (`/register`)
The user creates an account by providing email, password, first name, last name, date of birth, phone number, and optional drug allergy information. Passwords must meet complexity requirements (uppercase, lowercase, digit, special character, minimum 8 characters). An SMS consent checkbox is presented.

### Step 2: Membership Payment (`/subscribe`)
After registration, the user is directed to the subscription page to activate their $99/year membership. Payment is processed via Stripe (credit/debit card). The membership fee covers platform access and wholesale pricing — it does not cover the cost of individual medications or shipping.

### Step 3: Member Dashboard (`/dashboard`)
Upon successful payment, the user lands on the member dashboard. The dashboard shows:
- Subscription status (Active/Pending)
- Quick actions: Cost Calculator, My Medications, Prescription Request, Referrals, Browse Medications, Account Settings
- Primary care physician on file
- Drug allergy list
- Recent activity feed

### Step 4: Browse Medications (`/medications`)
The member browses the generic medication catalog (4,700+ FDA-approved generics). Each medication displays: name, strength, dosage form, manufacturer, wholesale price, and retail comparison price. Controlled substances are excluded from the catalog entirely.

### Step 5: Cost Calculator (`/cost-calculator`)
The member uses the interactive cost calculator to estimate their annual costs. The calculator factors in: $99 membership fee + ($10 dispensing fee per medication per fill) + ($5 shipping per order). Supply duration options: 30, 60, 90, 180, or 365 days.

### Step 6: Prescription Request (`/prescription-request`)
The member submits a prescription request by providing:
- Patient name, date of birth
- Medication name, dosage, quantity
- Prescribing doctor name, phone, fax, address
- Urgency level (routine/urgent/emergency)
- Special instructions

PDC generates a branded PDF and faxes it to the prescriber's office for authorization. The member can also download the PDF and text it to their phone.

### Step 7: Prescription Verification
HealthWarehouse receives the authorized prescription from the prescriber and performs:
- Prescription validity verification
- Drug utilization review (DUR) by a licensed pharmacist
- Drug-drug interaction screening
- Allergy cross-reference

### Step 8: Order Placement (`/checkout`)
Once the prescription is verified, the member places an order. The checkout page displays:
- Medication items with wholesale pricing
- $5 flat-rate shipping fee
- Shipping address (on file or new entry)
- Order summary with total

### Step 9: Fulfillment & Shipping
HealthWarehouse dispenses the medication and ships via USPS or UPS. The member receives:
- Order confirmation email
- Shipping confirmation with tracking number
- SMS notification (if opted in)

### Step 10: Order Tracking (`/orders`)
The member tracks their order status through the Orders page:
- Pending, Processing, Shipped, Delivered
- Tracking number with carrier link
- Estimated delivery date

### Step 11: Refill Management (`/refills`)
When a prescription allows refills, the member can request refills through the platform. Refill requests are routed to HealthWarehouse within the bounds of the original prescription authorization.
